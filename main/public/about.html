About Me

My name is Henry, and I am a tech enthusiast who’s passionate about privacy and security in our modern digital age. I am involved with technology, running, playing music, and managing an online community where we fight for our right to privacy and security. Currently, I am a full-time student and proud owner of Techlore.

About Techlore

My channel is a huge part of life—not only because it’s currently my main career, but everything I discuss on Techlore is related to something I am passionate about. I started the channel in December, 2014 with a guide on how to setup a free VPN for iPhones and iPads, and I’ve continued to grow the channel since then. My goal with Techlore is to spread awareness and offer a centralized place for information related to basic privacy and security while using the internet. We have an amazing community, so make sure to subscribe to us on YouTube if you want to be a part of the revolution!

About Go Incognito

Go Incognito is a video-based course teaching users of varying experience levels about privacy, security, and anonymity. It’s a major part of my goal to educate users around the world and provide them with not only a tutorial for improving their data habits, but to transform a user’s way of thinking about why, where, when, and how to treat their data and personal information. To top it off, Go Incognito is available free to watch, is shareable to the public through a Creative Commons license, and even offers a premium version for users who want a few more perks. It’s been in production since May, 2018, and is still in the process of being released. Track its progress here.
